Objectives of the event:

"AI For Emerging Trends and Technologies in Education" was a special workshop powered by Acer and Microsoft. The workshop enlightened the audience on the impact of the availability and affordability of good technology for AI development and how Acer is providing the common man access to such technology. Faculty and students were also educated on various ways in which they can leverage Microsoft Copilot in their particular areas of interest to maximize their productivity and improve their performance.

Outcomes of the event:

"AI For Emerging Trends and Technologies in Education" educated both the students and faculty on the emergence of Artificial Intelligence as an asset to education, and how these individuals can leverage AI-based technologies like Microsoft Copilot to enhance their learning and teaching techniques.

Benefits of the event:

Empowering students with AI knowledge to enhance learning and teaching methods.
