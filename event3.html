Objectives of the event:

"AI Renaissance: Cultivating the Seeds of Tomorrow's Tech" is a pillar of our continuing endeavors to bring pioneering minds together to talk about the emerging area of artificial intelligence and its enormous implications. The occasion featured a project presentation by several brilliant Chitkara University students. In addition, four special guests shared their extensive experience on a range of AI-related topics with us. The topics of discussion included the recent rise of artificial intelligence, its economic potential, its impact on human cognizance, the opportunities it has generated for employment, the hazards it poses, and the regulations that will mitigate these risks while preserving a sustainable way of life.

Outcomes of the event:

"AI Renaissance: Cultivating the Seeds of Tomorrow's Tech" has equipped students with wisdom on several AI-related topics like its history, its economic potential, and its potential emergence as a cognitive marvel. Its effect on human cognizance, and the ethics necessary for AI development. Students also realized the skill requirements in this job market. Numerous young talents were also enabled to showcase their initiated projects at this event. By the end of the program, the speakers had inspired the students to pursue a career in AI with adaptability as a key skill and to contribute to its advancement.

Benefits of the event:

Empowering AI Talent, Inspiring Innovations.
