A Legacy of Evolve AI & A look to the future

Our Origin

EvolveAI began its journey as a club consisting of ambitious team members within Chitkara University's Department of Computer Science & Engineering (Artificial Intelligence) in 2021. It is guided by visionaries like Dr. Sushil Kumar Narang and driven by dedicated individuals like Dr. Kamal Deep Garg and Dr. Vandana Sood. EvolveAI aspires to nurture a vibrant community centered around Artificial Intelligence. Our motive is to empower future AI leaders by offering the students exceptional opportunities for learning, growth, and contribution in the dynamic realm of AI.

Our Vision

We believe fostering collaboration and innovation in students is essential for them to thrive in the AI landscape. We want to help students achieve it through massive initiatives such as technical workshops, hackathons, competitions, and other interactive sessions. EvolveAI aims to cultivate a culture of perpetual learning and exploration among AI enthusiasts. The club believes that with the right guidance, resources, and mentorship, it can endeavor to equip students with the skills, knowledge, and connections necessary to emerge proficiently as AI leaders and practitioners. EvolveAI envisions a future where its members confidently lead the charge in AI research, engineering, and product development, catalyzing innovation and imaginative applications for the advancement of society.

Our Contemporary

In the present-day scenario, EvolveAI remains a vibrant epicentre for AI enthusiasts. The club is actively working on upholding its steadfast commitment to organizing events, fostering collaborations, and engaging with industry professionals to not only retain but also upgrade its position as a frontrunner in AI education and innovation. We all are living in an era where AI is revolutionising every sector, EvolveAI assumes a pivotal role in shaping the next cohort of AI pioneers. To accomplish it we as a team has been working on the simple yet effective approach of facilitating access to cutting-edge resources, advocating for interdisciplinary learning, and cultivating a nurturing ecosystem, empowering students to pursue careers in AI research, engineering, and entrepreneurship. As the demand for AI expertise continues its ascent, EvolveAI remains steadfast in its dedication to inspiring and preparing students to make impactful contributions to the realm of Artificial Intelligence.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is evolve AI currently upto?

- Evolve AI seeks collaboration with top-tier companies and industry experts.

- Aim to mentor and support young AI enthusiasts in the community.

- Offer workshops, expert talks, and projects for skill development.

- Encourage guidance and nurturing of talent in the field of AI.

Who can be a member at Evolve AI?

Is AI your passion? Whether it is your ambition to research and unvield revolutionary breakthroughs, or your skills and knowledge in developing AI-ML models that can benefit society, as long as you possess the skillset and have a desire to learn and engage with us, you are welcome to the community!

What has been Evolve AI's biggest achievement yet?

- Industrial collaboration with Ikigai Labs: Establishing a groundbreaking Computer Vision workshop, setting a new standard in collaboration between academia and industry.

- AI.Create() awareness campaign: Spreading knowledge about Generative AI, contributing to the democratization of AI technologies and fostering innovation.

- Founding of the AI Club: Pioneering the first AI-focused community at Chitkara University, fostering a culture of AI-driven problem-solving and project development.

- Central hub for AI projects: Positioning the AI Club as the go-to platform for tackling significant challenges through AI, consolidating expertise and resources for impactful solutions.

After becoming a member, can I be guided on my future projects?

Evolve AI team would feel delighted to guide all the tech enthusiastic members with their future projects. This club is not just an experience but also a community to foster collaboration, knowledge sharing, and skill development.

How can new members actively contribute to the growth and success of Evolve AI?

You should accomplish all the tasks given to you on a satisfactory level. Also, by regularly attending club meetings, workshops, and events. This not only keeps you informed about club activities but also provides opportunities to connect with other members and share your perspectives.
