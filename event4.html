Objectives of the event:

AI-CREATE 2.0 was a contest that encouraged students from diverse backgrounds, such as AI and Design, to come together and explore new horizons in technology while examining the intersections of human creativity and artificial intelligence, enhancing each other in innovative ways. This collaboration served as a reminder that while technology continually advances, it is in synergy with human creativity that we can achieve truly remarkable and imaginative outcomes. The winners of this contest were presented with cash prices.

Outcomes of the event:

AI-CREATE 2.0 was a contest which successfully demonstrated how combining artificial intelligence and human creativity can lead to exciting innovations. The images created by students in this collaborative event showed the great potential of working together. It reminded us that technology and human imagination can make an excellent team, and it left everyone inspired to keep exploring these exciting possibilities. This event celebrated the students' achievements and sparked a newfound enthusiasm for working together across different fields, paving the way for a promising future where technology and creative thinking go hand in hand.
