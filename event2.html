Objectives of the event:

“Intersect: Confluence of Ideas- A Multidisciplinary Poster Presentation Showcasing Innovative Solutions using Design Thinking and Critical Thinking” was a golden opportunity for all the departments from the university to represent their department and their ideas in front of an esteemed jury. They proposed solutions inculcating Artificial Intelligence to the problems from their own departments showcasing their skill full mindset. It was a platform for them to present the solutions of problems with creative-thinking and problem-solution ability enriching the academic landscape and fostering a culture of innovation within the university. This event helped enrich the academic landscape of the university.

Outcomes of the event:

“Intersect: Confluence of Ideas- A Multidisciplinary Poster Presentation Showcasing Innovative Solutions using Design Thinking and Critical Thinking " has equipped students with wisdom on several AI-related solutions of problems in their respective disciplines. This event resulted in cross-pollination of ideas, explore new avenues and networking opportunities with fellow participants and professionals. This was a platform for them to showcase their creativity and problem-solving skills facilitating deeper understanding of their own field and how Artificial intelligence can be a solution to the problems in their field.

Benefits of the event:

Empowering AI Talent, Inspiring Innovations.
